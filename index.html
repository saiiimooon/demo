1. Feet more than the shoulder width apart, hands down by the side in blades.

  • a) Cheer Stance
  • b) High V
  • c) Low V
  • d) Clasp

Answer: a) Cheer Stance

2. Arms extended down forming a “V”

  • a) Cheer Stance
  • b) High V
  • c) Low V
  • d) Clasp

Answer: c) Low V

3. Hands in blades, at the chin, elbows in

  • a) Cheer Stance
  • b) High V
  • c) Clap
  • d) Clasp

Answer: c) Clap

4. Hands __ at the chin, elbows in.

  • a) Cheer Stance
  • b) High V
  • c) Clap
  • d) Clasp

Answer: d) Clasp

5. Arms extended up forming a “V”, relax the shoulders.

  • a) Cheer Stance
  • b) High V
  • c) Clap
  • d) Clasp

Answer: b) High V
